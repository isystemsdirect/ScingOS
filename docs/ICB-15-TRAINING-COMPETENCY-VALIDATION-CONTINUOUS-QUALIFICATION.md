**STATUS:** CANONICAL / INHERITS ICB-01 THROUGH ICB-14 / NON-OPTIONAL

---

## 1. PURPOSE

ICB-15 defines how SCINGULAR:

- Ensures inspector competency
- Validates ongoing qualification
- Enforces training requirements
- Maintains professional readiness
- Reduces systemic inspection risk

This block ensures human capability matches system authority.

---

## 2. TRAINING CATEGORIES (CANONICAL)

SCINGULAR SHALL recognize training in:

- Inspection domain knowledge
- Applicable codes and standards
- Jurisdiction-specific requirements
- Evidence capture and documentation
- Ethics and professional conduct
- System usage and security
- Safety and hazard recognition

---

## 3. INITIAL QUALIFICATION REQUIREMENTS

Before authorization, inspectors SHALL:

- Complete required baseline training
- Demonstrate understanding of scope
- Pass competency validation
- Acknowledge ethical obligations

**FAILURE TO QUALIFY SHALL BLOCK INSPECTION AUTHORITY.**

---

## 4. CONTINUING EDUCATION

SCINGULAR SHALL:

- Track continuing education hours
- Enforce renewal cycles
- Align training with jurisdictional mandates
- Require updates when codes change

**LAPSED EDUCATION SHALL SUSPEND AUTHORIZATION.**

---

## 5. COMPETENCY VALIDATION METHODS

Validation MAY include:

- Examinations
- Scenario-based assessments
- Field performance review
- Audit findings analysis
- Supervisor evaluations

Validation results SHALL be recorded and auditable.

---

## 6. DOMAIN & SCOPE ALIGNMENT

Training SHALL be:

- Domain-specific
- Scope-matched
- Jurisdiction-aware

Inspectors MAY NOT operate outside trained domains.

---

## 7. PERFORMANCE MONITORING

SCINGULAR SHALL:

- Track inspection outcomes
- Monitor error and rework rates
- Identify training gaps
- Flag performance anomalies

Recurring issues SHALL trigger remediation.

---

## 8. REMEDIATION & RE-QUALIFICATION

When deficiencies are identified:

- Targeted training SHALL be assigned
- Authorization MAY be limited or suspended
- Re-validation SHALL be required

---

## 9. TRAINING RECORD MANAGEMENT

SCINGULAR SHALL:

- Store training records immutably
- Associate records with inspector profile
- Retain records per legal requirements
- Support audit and authority review

---

## 10. SYSTEM ENFORCEMENT

SCINGULAR SHALL:

- Block inspections without current qualification
- Enforce training prerequisites
- Log all training and validation events
- Preserve full competency history

---

END ICB-15
