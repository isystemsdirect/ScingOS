# Investor & Board Summary — Training & Compliance Framework (v1.0)

**Company:** Inspection Systems Direct LLC (ISD-C)  
**Brand:** SCINGULAR®  
**Classification:** Shareable under NDA / diligence

---

## Overview
The company enforces a mandatory, auditable workforce training program designed to protect intellectual property, reduce security and operational risk, and scale personnel governance.

## Key Controls
- Mandatory onboarding completion within 30 days
- Annual security and compliance renewals
- Role-based access tied to training status
- Centralized tracking and audit evidence
- Executive enforcement authority

## Business Impact
- Strengthened IP protection and trade-secret hygiene
- Reduced likelihood of incidents and liability exposure
- Repeatable operational quality and documentation discipline
- Diligence-ready governance posture
