# Training Course Catalog & Syllabi (v1.0)

**Company:** Inspection Systems Direct LLC (ISD-C)  
**Brand:** SCINGULAR®  
**Program:** SCINGULAR Workforce Training & Compliance Framework  
**Classification:** Proprietary & Confidential — Internal Use Only

---

## Course 1 — Core Orientation Training
**Audience:** All personnel  
**Duration:** ~2 hours  
**Deadline:** Within 7 calendar days of start date  
**Modules:**
- Company mission, vision, values
- Org structure & reporting
- Workplace conduct & ethics
- Safety & emergency procedures  
**Assessment:** Quiz (Pass ≥ 85%)

---

## Course 2 — Security & Data Protection
**Audience:** All personnel with data/system access  
**Duration:** ~2.5 hours  
**Deadline:** Within 14 calendar days of start date  
**Renewal:** Annual  
**Modules:**
- Data classification & handling
- Confidentiality obligations
- Cyber hygiene (phishing, credentials, device security)
- Incident reporting & escalation  
**Assessment:** Scenario-based evaluation

---

## Course 3 — Role-Specific Technical Training
**Audience:** Role-dependent  
**Duration:** Variable  
**Deadline:** Within 30 calendar days of start date  
**Modules:**
- Required tools & platforms
- SOPs and workflows
- Documentation and quality standards
- Role-specific compliance requirements  
**Assessment:** Practical demo or manager sign-off

---

## Course 4 — Compliance & Legal Awareness
**Audience:** All personnel  
**Duration:** ~1.5 hours  
**Deadline:** Within 30 calendar days of start date  
**Renewal:** Annual or upon policy revision  
**Modules:**
- Intellectual property ownership and protection
- Conflicts of interest
- Code of conduct acknowledgment
- Regulatory exposure awareness  
**Assessment:** Acknowledgment + quiz
