## 1. PURPOSE

ICB-10 defines the ethical boundaries and professional conduct requirements enforced by SCINGULAR to ensure:

- Inspection integrity
- Public trust
- Regulatory credibility
- Legal defensibility
- Investor-grade governance

This block prevents bias, abuse, and conflict.

---

## 2. CORE ETHICAL PRINCIPLES

All inspections SHALL be governed by:

- Objectivity
- Independence
- Transparency
- Consistency
- Accountability

No business, financial, or personal interest may influence inspection outcomes.

---

## 3. CONFLICT OF INTEREST DEFINITIONS

A conflict exists when an inspector or operator:

- Has financial interest in the inspected entity
- Has personal or familial relationship with stakeholders
- Provides consulting, design, or repair services for the site
- Receives gifts, incentives, or preferential treatment
- Is subject to coercion or undue influence

---

## 4. DISCLOSURE & DECLARATION RULES

SCINGULAR SHALL require:

- Pre-inspection conflict declarations
- Ongoing disclosure of new conflicts
- Authority notification when conflicts exist

**UNDECLARED CONFLICTS INVALIDATE INSPECTION RESULTS.**

---

## 5. PROHIBITED CONDUCT

Inspectors SHALL NOT:

- Accept gifts or compensation beyond authorized fees
- Provide design, repair, or remediation services
- Alter findings under pressure
- Conceal violations
- Bypass system safeguards
- Share confidential data improperly

---

## 6. PROFESSIONAL COMMUNICATION STANDARDS

Inspectors SHALL:

- Use factual, neutral language
- Avoid speculative statements
- Avoid guarantees or assurances
- Clearly state scope limitations
- Communicate risks without exaggeration

---

## 7. ENFORCEMENT & DISCIPLINE

When ethical violations occur:

- Inspection SHALL be flagged
- Findings SHALL be reviewed
- Authority SHALL be notified
- Credentials MAY be suspended or revoked
- Records SHALL be preserved

---

## 8. WHISTLEBLOWER & INTEGRITY SAFEGUARDS

SCINGULAR SHALL:

- Allow confidential reporting of misconduct
- Protect reporters from retaliation
- Log and investigate allegations
- Preserve evidence and records

---

## 9. TRAINING & ACKNOWLEDGEMENT

Inspectors SHALL:

- Complete ethics training
- Acknowledge code of conduct
- Re-certify understanding periodically

Training records are auditable.

---

## 10. SYSTEM ENFORCEMENT

SCINGULAR SHALL:

- Block inspections with unresolved conflicts
- Require disclosures before execution
- Log all ethics-related events
- Preserve immutable ethics records

---

END ICB-10
