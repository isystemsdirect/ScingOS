# ICB-12 — AUDIT, OVERSIGHT & REGULATORY READINESS

**STATUS:** CANONICAL / INHERITS ICB-01 THROUGH ICB-11 / NON-OPTIONAL

---

## 1. PURPOSE

ICB-12 defines how SCINGULAR:

- Supports internal and external audits
- Enables regulatory oversight
- Maintains inspection transparency
- Preserves long-term defensibility
- Demonstrates compliance maturity

This block makes SCINGULAR audit-ready by design.

---

## 2. AUDIT TYPES (CANONICAL)

SCINGULAR SHALL support:

- Internal quality audits
- Regulatory audits
- Third-party certification audits
- Legal and litigation audits
- Investor and due-diligence audits

All audits SHALL be traceable to source records.

---

## 3. AUDIT SCOPE DEFINITION

Audits MAY target:

- Individual inspections
- Inspector activity
- Jurisdiction logic
- Evidence integrity
- Report accuracy
- Enforcement actions
- System configuration

Scope SHALL be logged and preserved.

---

## 4. AUDIT ACCESS CONTROLS

SCINGULAR SHALL:

- Provide read-only audit access
- Restrict modification during audits
- Log all auditor activity
- Enforce least-privilege principles

**NO AUDITOR MAY ALTER ORIGINAL RECORDS.**

---

## 5. TRACEABILITY REQUIREMENTS

SCINGULAR SHALL maintain end-to-end traceability between:

- Trigger → inspection
- Inspection → evidence
- Evidence → findings
- Findings → corrective actions
- Corrective actions → closure

**BROKEN TRACEABILITY IS A SYSTEM FAILURE.**

---

## 6. OVERSIGHT & REVIEW MECHANISMS

SCINGULAR SHALL support:

- Supervisor review workflows
- Authority review checkpoints
- Secondary validation for critical findings
- Randomized inspection sampling

Review outcomes SHALL be logged.

---

## 7. REGULATORY INQUIRY & RESPONSE

When subject to regulatory inquiry:

- Requested records SHALL be retrievable
- Response timelines SHALL be tracked
- Submissions SHALL be logged
- Originals SHALL be preserved

**NO AD-HOC RECORD CREATION IS PERMITTED.**

---

## 8. METRICS & COMPLIANCE INDICATORS

SCINGULAR SHALL produce:

- Inspection volume metrics
- Violation trends
- Repeat non-compliance indicators
- Enforcement effectiveness measures
- Inspector performance statistics

Metrics SHALL be derived from source data only.

---

## 9. CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT FEEDBACK

Audit findings MAY:

- Trigger system configuration updates
- Inform training needs
- Adjust risk weighting
- Improve inspection logic

All changes SHALL be versioned and justified.

---

## 10. SYSTEM ENFORCEMENT

SCINGULAR SHALL:

- Prevent audit circumvention
- Preserve immutable audit trails
- Enforce transparency by default
- Support long-term regulatory trust

---

END ICB-12
