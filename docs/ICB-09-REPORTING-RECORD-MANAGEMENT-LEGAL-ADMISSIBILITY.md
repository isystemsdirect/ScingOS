## 1. PURPOSE

ICB-09 defines how SCINGULAR:

- Generates inspection reports
- Manages official records
- Ensures legal admissibility
- Controls amendments and versions
- Supports audits, subpoenas, and litigation

This block makes outputs defensible.

---

## 2. REPORT TYPES (CANONICAL)

SCINGULAR SHALL support the following report types:

- Initial inspection report
- Re-inspection / verification report
- Partial compliance report
- Non-compliance report
- Imminent danger / emergency report
- Closure report
- Supplemental / addendum report

Each report type has fixed required fields.

---

## 3. MANDATORY REPORT CONTENT

All reports SHALL include:

- Issuing authority and jurisdiction
- Unique report ID
- Inspection ID and trigger
- Inspection date(s) and time(s)
- Site location and scope
- Inspector(s) identity and credentials
- Applicable codes and editions
- Findings and observations
- Violation listings with severity
- Corrective actions and deadlines
- Enforcement status
- Disclaimers and scope limitations
- Authorized signature

**MISSING REQUIRED CONTENT INVALIDATES THE REPORT.**

---

## 4. STRUCTURE & FORMAT RULES

Reports SHALL:

- Separate facts from notes
- Avoid design or engineering advice
- Use objective, neutral language
- Reference evidence explicitly
- Be machine-readable and human-readable

Formatting SHALL be consistent across reports.

---

## 5. VERSIONING & AMENDMENTS

Once issued:

- Reports are locked
- Original version is preserved

Amendments SHALL:

- Create a new version
- Reference the prior version
- State reason for amendment
- Be time-stamped
- Identify authorizing party

**NO CONTENT MAY BE DELETED OR OVERWRITTEN.**

---

## 6. RECORD RETENTION & STORAGE

SCINGULAR SHALL:

- Store reports and evidence immutably
- Apply jurisdictional retention schedules
- Prevent unauthorized deletion
- Maintain redundancy and backups

Retention SHALL follow the strictest applicable law.

---

## 7. LEGAL ADMISSIBILITY PRINCIPLES

All records SHALL be:

- Authentic
- Accurate
- Complete
- Traceable
- Tamper-evident

SCINGULAR SHALL support:

- Chain-of-custody
- Access logs
- Export for legal proceedings

---

## 8. PUBLIC RECORDS & CONFIDENTIALITY

When subject to public records laws:

- Records SHALL be retrievable
- Redactions SHALL be logged
- Confidential data SHALL be protected per law

**CONFIDENTIALITY SHALL NOT DEFEAT DISCOVERY.**

---

## 9. ACCESS CONTROL & AUTHORIZATION

SCINGULAR SHALL:

- Restrict report access by role
- Log all access events
- Prevent unauthorized sharing
- Support read-only legal access

Access logs are immutable.

---

## 10. SYSTEM ENFORCEMENT

SCINGULAR SHALL:

- Block issuance of non-compliant reports
- Enforce version control
- Preserve permanent audit trails
- Support regulatory and judicial review

---

END ICB-09
