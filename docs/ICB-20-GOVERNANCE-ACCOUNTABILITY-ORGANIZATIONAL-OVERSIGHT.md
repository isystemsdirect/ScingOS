## 1. PURPOSE

ICB-20 defines how SCINGULAR:

- Establishes governance authority
- Assigns accountability
- Controls decision ownership
- Separates duties
- Demonstrates organizational responsibility

This block ensures the system has clear human ownership.

---

## 2. GOVERNANCE STRUCTURE

SCINGULAR SHALL operate under a defined governance model including:

- System owner
- Compliance authority
- Technical steward
- Security officer
- Audit & oversight function

Roles SHALL be formally appointed and documented.

---

## 3. DECISION OWNERSHIP

Every material decision SHALL have:

- A named decision owner
- Defined authority scope
- Accountability for outcomes

**DECISIONS WITHOUT OWNERSHIP ARE INVALID.**

---

## 4. SEPARATION OF DUTIES

SCINGULAR SHALL enforce separation between:

- Inspection execution
- Review and approval
- Enforcement authorization
- System administration
- Audit functions

**NO SINGLE ROLE MAY CONTROL END-TO-END PROCESS.**

---

## 5. OVERSIGHT MECHANISMS

Governance SHALL include:

- Periodic management review
- Compliance review cycles
- Audit committee oversight
- Risk review sessions

Oversight outcomes SHALL be logged.

---

## 6. POLICY MANAGEMENT

SCINGULAR SHALL:

- Maintain formal policies
- Version policies
- Bind policies to system behavior
- Require acknowledgment by users

Policy violations SHALL be tracked.

---

## 7. ESCALATION & DECISION REVIEW

When disputes or failures occur:

- Escalation paths SHALL be defined
- Review authority SHALL be identified
- Decisions SHALL be revisitable
- Outcomes SHALL be documented

---

## 8. PERFORMANCE & ACCOUNTABILITY METRICS

Governance SHALL monitor:

- Compliance rates
- Inspection quality indicators
- Enforcement effectiveness
- Incident frequency
- Audit findings

Metrics SHALL inform governance actions.

---

## 9. EXTERNAL OVERSIGHT & REPORTING

SCINGULAR SHALL support:

- Regulatory reporting
- Investor due diligence
- Independent assessments
- Public accountability where required

Reporting SHALL use verified data only.

---

## 10. SYSTEM ENFORCEMENT

SCINGULAR SHALL:

- Block actions outside governance authority
- Enforce role accountability
- Preserve governance records
- Support audit and legal review

---

END ICB-20
